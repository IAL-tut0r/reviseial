Hi, I'm Abdur.

I built ReviseIAL to help fellow A-Level students get better at exam technique — one topic at a time.

💡 Why I built ReviseIAL

As an Edexcel International A-Level student, I found it frustrating that past paper questions were scattered across dozens of papers with no easy way to practise a specific topic. If you're weak on Kinetics or Waves, you'd have to dig through years of papers just to find relevant questions.

ReviseIAL fixes that. Every question is organised by topic, tagged by difficulty, and comes with a worked solution so you don't just get the answer — you understand the method.

✏️ Feel free to personalise this section with your own words when you're ready!

🎯 What ReviseIAL aims to be

A free, no-login, no-nonsense revision tool for Edexcel IAL Chemistry and Physics students worldwide. No ads, no paywalls — just good questions and clear solutions.

Currently covering IAS (Year 1) for both subjects, with IAL2 (Year 2) coming soon.
